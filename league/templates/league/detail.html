Tips & Info

General

  • Click the banner at the top of the screen to return to the home page
  • You can search for a Classic or a Head-to-Head league but only the top 50 players will be shown
  • Bookmark the search result so you don't need to re-enter your Manager or League ID in future. You can also share that link with your friends so that they can bypass the search and go straight to the results page
  • Every column in the main tables can be sorted to quickly find the highest/lowest values for any category

Manager Stats

  • Click on the Manager's name in the top left corner to go to that manager's team page on the main FPL website
  • Click on the week number in any of the main tables to see the manager's team for that week on the main FPL website
  • Under 'Team Selection' you can click on the number in the Max Points column to see the optimal team lineup for that week

League Stats

  • Click on the League name in the top left corner to go to that league's page on the main FPL website
  • You can click on a Manager's name in any of the the main tables to go to the Manager Stats view for that person

General Statistics

Number/Points

  • Use these buttons to toggle between Number View and Points View. Number View shows you how many of each category has been scored while Points View shows you how many points have been gained or lost in each category.

Manager

  • The Manager's name.

Base Points

  • Total points before the Captain multiplier is applied or deductions for transfers are made.

Minutes

  • Total amount of minutes played by all players or points gained from minutes played.

Goals

  • Total goals scored by all players or total points gained from goals.

Assists

  • Total assists made by all players or total points gained from assists.

Clean Sheets

  • For Number View, shows the number of clean sheets kept by the Goalkeeper and Defenders.
    For Points View, shows the points obtained from clean sheets kept by the Goalkeeper, Defenders and Midfielders.

Saves

  • Total saves made by all players or total points gained from saves.

Goals Conceded

  • Total goals conceded by the Goalkeeper and Defenders or total points lost from goals conceded.

Penalties Saved

  • Total penalties saved by all players or total points gained from penalties saved.

Yellow Cards

  • Total yellow cards received by all players or total points lost from yellow cards.

Red Cards

  • Total red cards received by all players or total points lost from red cards.

Penalties Missed

  • Total penalties missed by all players or total points lost from penalties missed.

Own Goals

  • Total own goals scored by all players or total points lost from own goals.

Bonus Points

  • Total Bonus Points awarded to all players.

Team Selection

Manager

  • The Manager's name.

Transfer Cost

  • The amount of points spent on transfers.

Preferred Captain

  • The player who was chosen as Captain the most often. If the Vice Captain played and the Captain didn't play (or has yet to play in the current week), the Vice Captain is counted instead.

Captain Points

  • How many points have been acquired from Captains over the season.

Season MVP

  • The player who has scored the most points for the team, including points gained as Captain.

MVP Points

  • How many points the Season MVP has scored.

Captain Points Lost

  • How many points in total the team lost due to not selecting the highest scoring player as Captain each week.

Points on Bench

  • Total number of points scored by the players on the bench.

Bench Points Lost

  • Total points lost by not selecting the optimal starting eleven each week.

Points

  • Total points scored, including the Captain multiplier and deductions for transfers.

Maximum Points

  • The maximum amount of points that could have been obtained by choosing the optimal Captain and starting eleven every week.

Potential Lost

  • Total points lost by not choosing the optimal Captain and starting eleven every week.
