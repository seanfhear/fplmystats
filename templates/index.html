Tips & Info

General

  • Click the banner at the top of the screen to return to the home page
  • Every column in the main tables can be sorted to quickly find the highest/lowest values for any category
  • You can search for a Classic or a Head-to-Head league but only the top 50 players will be shown
  • Bookmark the search result so you don't need to re-enter your Manager or League ID in future. You can also share that link with your friends so that they can bypass the search and go straight to the results page
  • The site is best viewed in horizontal orientation. If you are on mobile, it can also help to select 'Request Desktop View' in your browser's options

Manager Stats

  • Click on the Manager's name in the top left corner to go to that manager's team page on the main FPL website
  • Click on the week number in any of the main tables to see the manager's team for that week on the main FPL website
  • Under 'Team Selection' you can click on the number in the Max Points column to see the optimal team lineup for that week

League Stats

  • Click on the League name in the top left corner to go to that league's page on the main FPL website
  • You can click on a Manager's name in any of the the main tables to go to the Manager Stats view for that person
